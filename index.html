"Why E-Waste is Crucial in India"
A. Environmental Concerns
Toxic Pollution: E-waste contains hazardous substances like lead, mercury, cadmium, and brominated flame retardants, which contaminate soil, air, and water. Air and Water Degradation: Burning or acid-leaching e-waste (common in informal sectors) releases carcinogenic fumes and contaminates local water bodies. Climate Impact: Poor disposal contributes to carbon emissions and long-term ecological imbalance.
